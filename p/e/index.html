About Us

2225 Highland Village Road
Highland Village, Texas 75077

Excite! first opened its doors in February 2001 in Flower Mound. Rapid growth and success of its programs made it necessary to purchase a second, larger facility. This is the location in Highland Village where programs including, pre-school, cheer, tumbling, gymnastics, Girl Scouts workshops, camps, clinics and “parent’s night out” are offered.

Excite! seeks to bring out the personal best in every participant. We give each individual the encouragement, training and attention necessary to achieve maximum success, whether it’s in our recreational classes or our competitive teams.
